Free Photo & Signature Resizer for Any Form

PhotoSign is a free online image resizer that lets you resize your photo or signature to the exact pixel dimensions and file size (in KB) required by any application form — without uploading your file to any server.

Commonly used for: SSC CGL photo resize · SSC CHSL signature resize · UPSC CSE photo size · NDA photo dimensions · AFCAT photo compress · RRB NTPC photo resize · IBPS PO photo size · SBI PO signature · NEET photo compress · JEE Main photo resize · CUET form photo · State PSC forms · Visa applications · College admission forms · Job portal profile photos

How it works: Upload your image → Enter the width and height in pixels → Select the maximum file size in KB → Click Resize & Compress → Download. Everything runs in your browser. Nothing is uploaded, stored, or shared.
